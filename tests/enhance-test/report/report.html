  1. AI/ML is the breakout story. Starting from a tiny base of 12,400 in 2020, AI/ML Engineer roles exploded to 158,700 by 2025 — a 12.8× increase and a 66% compound annual growth rate, dwarfing every other category.
  2. Cloud Engineering quadrupled. Cloud Engineer grew from 58,700 to 201,300 (3.4×), reflecting the continued migration of enterprise workloads to cloud infrastructure. Its 28% CAGR makes it the second-fastest growing role.
  3. Cybersecurity demand is accelerating. Cybersecurity Analyst positions nearly doubled from 141,200 to 268,200, growing at a steady 14% CAGR — consistent with rising threat landscapes and regulatory requirements.
  4. Software Development remains the foundation. While its 4.3% CAGR is the slowest of the five, Software Developer still added 434,100 net new jobs — the largest absolute increase of any role.
